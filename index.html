How to Use This Map

  • Zoom & Pan: Use your mouse wheel to zoom in and out. Click and drag the background to pan the view.
  • Arrange Nodes: Click and drag any company or project to pin it in place. This lets you untangle the graph and explore connections.
  • Release Node: Double-click a pinned node to release it back into the physics simulation.
  • View Details: Hover over a connection to highlight it, then click it to see more details and source links. Click the background to hide the details.
  • Reset: Click the "Reset View" button to un-pin all nodes and return to the default zoom.
